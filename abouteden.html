About Us

Our goal is to empower you, our client, to live a wholesome life despite your current medical situation.

Every person has unique needs, and we appreciate and respect this. Therefore, we can arrange an assessment and discuss how you can be best supported.

Our CEO, Corinne Felix, has been in the Care Industry for the past 14 years and has extensive experience in caring for elderly persons of varied cultures, backgrounds, and health needs. Her clients have included those who are challenged with Dementia, Cancer, Parkinson’s, Multiple Sclerosis, and diverse physical disabilities.

Eden’s Care Services’ passion and overarching goal is to support individuals in making positive changes, giving them the confidence to function within their homes, communities, and social circles with a measure of independence.
